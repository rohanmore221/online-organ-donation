ABOUT US

AKROSH company the organisation of ARA Foundation for charity is a Navi Mumbai based company that helps to ease the process of organ DONATION It also creates awareness to generate more number of people for organ donation.We provide information regarding the organ donation process to interested donors.We also,provide online updation of reports for patients, where patients can have easy live update of their reports. Doctors are allowed to enter patients report for any tests Conducted, he/she can also fetch patient history when a patient visits for routine checkups.For Creating Awareness we provide live news updates,Inspirational stories of organ donors and also the procedure of how organ can be donated.



OUR AIM IS TO MOTIVATE NORMAL HUMAN BEINGS TO PLEDGE TO DONATE ORGANS AFTER DEATH, AND TO SPREAD AWARENESS ABOUT THE IMPORTANCE OF ORGAN DONATION THE VALUE OF LIFE IS NOT IN ITS DURATION, BUT IN ITS DONATION. YOU ARE NOT IMPORTANT BECAUSE OF HOW LONG YOU LIVE, YOU ARE IMPORTANT BECAUSE OF HOW EFFECTIVE YOU LIVE.
